LaTeX, a Document Preparation System

by Stefan Kottwitz (Packt Publishing, 2011, ISBN-10 1847199860, ISBN-13 1847199860)
The LaTeX Companion
by Frank Mittelbach, Michel Goossens, Johannes Braams, David Carlisle and Chris Rowley (second edition, Addison-Wesley, 2004, ISBN-10 0-201-36299-6, ISBN-13 0-201-36299-6); the book as also available as a digital download (in EPUB, MOBI and PDF formats) from http://www.informit.com/store/latex-companion-9780133387667
The LaTeX Graphics Companion:
Illustrating documents with TeX and PostScript by Michel Goossens, Sebastian Rahtz, Frank Mittelbach, Denis Roegel and Herbert Voß[[[LBRACE]]][[[RBRACE]]] (second edition, Addison-Wesley, 2007, ISBN-10 0-321-50892-0, ISBN-13 0-321-50892-0)
The LaTeX Web Companion:

TeX Unbound:

More Math into LaTeX:
An Introduction to LaTeX and AMSLaTeX by George Gr\"atzer (fourth edition Springer Verlag, 2007, ISBN-10 978-0-387-32289-6
Digital Typography Using LaTeX

First Steps in LaTeX
by George Gr\"atzer (Birkh\"auser, 1999, ISBN-10 0-8176-4132-7)
LaTeX: Line by Line:

LaTeX for Linux:
A Vade Mecum by Bernice Sacks Lipkin (Springer-Verlag, 1999, ISBN-10 0-387-98708-8, second printing)
Typesetting Mathematics with LaTeX

Typesetting Tables with LaTeX
