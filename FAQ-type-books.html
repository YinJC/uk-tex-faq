Books on Type

The following is a partial listing of books on typography in general. Of these, Bringhurst seems to be the one most often recommended.

The Elements of Typographic Style
by Robert Bringhurst (Hartley \& Marks, 1992, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]0-88179-033-8[[[RBRACE]]])
Finer Points in the Spacing \& Arrangement of Type
by Geoffrey Dowding (Hartley \& Marks, 1996, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]0-88179-119-9[[[RBRACE]]])
The Thames \& Hudson Manual of Typography
by Ruari McLean (Thames \& Hudson, 1980, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]0-500-68022-1[[[RBRACE]]])
The Form of the Book
by Jan Tschichold (Lund Humphries, 1991, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]0-85331-623-6[[[RBRACE]]])
Type \& Layout
by Colin Wheildon (Strathmore Press, 2006, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]1-875750-22-3[[[RBRACE]]])
The Design of Books
by Adrian Wilson (Chronicle Books, 1993, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]0-8118-0304-X[[[RBRACE]]])
Optical Letter Spacing
by David Kindersley and Lida Cardozo Kindersley (The Cardozo Kindersley Workshop 2001, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]1-874426-139[[[RBRACE]]])

There are many catalogues of type specimens but the following books provide a more interesting overall view of types in general and some of their history.

Alphabets Old \& New
by Lewis F. Day (Senate, 1995, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]1-85958-160-9[[[RBRACE]]])
An Introduction to the History of Printing Types
by Geoffrey Dowding (British Library, 1998, UK [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]0-7123-4563-9[[[RBRACE]]]; USA [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]1-884718-44-2[[[RBRACE]]])
The Alphabet Abecedarium
by Richard A. Firmage (David R. Godine, 1993, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]0-87923-998-0[[[RBRACE]]])
The Alphabet and Elements of Lettering
by Frederick Goudy (Dover, 1963, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]0-486-20792-7[[[RBRACE]]])
Anatomy of a Typeface
by Alexander Lawson (David R. Godine, 1990, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]0-87923-338-8[[[RBRACE]]])
A Tally of Types
by Stanley Morison (David R. Godine, 1999, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]1-56792-004-7[[[RBRACE]]])
Counterpunch
by Fred Smeijers (Hyphen, 1996, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]0-907259-06-5[[[RBRACE]]])
Treasury of Alphabets and Lettering
by Jan Tschichold (W. W. Norton, 1992, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]0-393-70197-2[[[RBRACE]]])
A Short History of the Printed Word
by Warren Chappell and Robert Bringhurst (Hartley \& Marks, 1999, [[[ISBN]]][[[LBRACE]]]0-88179-154-7[[[RBRACE]]])

The above lists are limited to books published in English. Typographic styles are somewhat language-dependent, and similarly the ‘interesting’ fonts depend on the particular writing system involved.
